IonSat is recruiting !

IonSat is recruiting !

   |  1 minutes  |  102 words  |  Antoine Tavant  • Other languages: Fr

Take the direction of a satellite

Ionsat is a 6U nanosatellite equipped with an electric propulsion engine, dedicated to demonstrating the feasibility of nanosatellite missions in very low orbit (250km).

Florian Marmuse, who is at the origin of Ionsat, is moving on to other horizons after four years at the head of the project. We are therefore recruiting a project manager with a strong systems engineering component and expertise in electronics and embedded systems.

You will find all the information on our job offer that can be found here .

Are you interested in the project? Do not hesitate to contact us!
