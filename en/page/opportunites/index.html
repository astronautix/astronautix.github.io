Interships and Opportunities

Take part of the space adventure !

Join us

Because of our many projects, there are many opportunities to join us! There is bellow a shot list of the differents opportunities to join us in our adventure. Feel free to contact us, we would love to discuss with you and maybe create a warm place for you within us.

Participate in our projects

We offer internships to work on our Nanosatellites projects Ionsat and Crocus.

Internships can cover a wide range of subjects: electronics and on-board systems, attitude control, orbit control, etc.

We offer specific internships for IonSat. More information on the dedicated page

Do not hesitate to contact us to build a subject together!

Come and supervise our projects

The CSEP is based on student projects supervised by Ph.D. students on complementary missions. The supervision mission requires on average half a day per week during the academic year.

We are looking for all types of profiles: mechanics, electronics, automation, etc. If you are a PhD student near Palaiseau, and if the space excites you, do not hesitate to contact us!

Other opportunities

We propose to help students in their search for projects, internships or jobs in the space sector. Below, you will find various websites referring companies or internship proposals. This page is updated regularly.

The Exploration Company

Here at the The Exploration Company we are developing, producing, and operating Nyx, a modular and reusable space orbital vehicle that can eventually be refueled in orbit, and which carries cargo - and potentially humans in the longer run. We are democratising space exploration i.e., by making it affordable, available, and open.

All our internship and job offerings are available online.

Application on this link. Or you can visit the company website: https://www.exploration.space/

ThalesAlenia Space

As a sponsor of the teaching programme Espace, Sciences et défis du Spatial, ThalesAlenia Space offers internships for students of École polytechnique.

List of European SMEs

ESA collects and collates a wealth of information on European space SMEs. The complete database is on this site.
